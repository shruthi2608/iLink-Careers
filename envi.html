Job Title: Environment Engineer - Application Monitoring and Infrastructure Management

This is a dynamic role where you'll be responsible for monitoring and analyzing environmental systems, implementing monitoring tools, troubleshooting issues, and contributing to environmental sustainability.

Responsibilities:

  • Monitor and analyze environmental systems, focusing on applications and infrastructure.
  • Implement and maintain monitoring tools.
  • Troubleshoot and resolve issues related to performance and stability.
  • Design, implement, and manage scalable infrastructure solutions.
  • Conduct assessments and audits to identify improvement areas.
  • Develop and execute data collection, analysis, and reporting strategies.
  • Stay current with industry trends and best practices.
  • Contribute to environmental sustainability efforts.

Skills and Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in engineering, computer science, or related field.
  • Experience in application monitoring and infrastructure management.
  • Knowledge of monitoring tools (e.g., Nagios, Prometheus).
  • Proficiency in infrastructure management (cloud, on-premises).
  • Troubleshooting and problem-solving skills.
  • Familiarity with environmental regulations (plus).
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
  • Proactive approach to identifying and addressing issues.
